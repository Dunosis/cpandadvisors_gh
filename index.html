About Our Firm

Claudia Principal & Associates is an accounting firm in St. Louis, Missouri, dedicated to supporting the Hispanic community and small and medium-sized businesses. We focus on providing comprehensive accounting and financial advisory solutions to help our clients achieve their goals and strengthen their businesses. With a deep commitment to the Latino community, we offer personalized, accessible services in their language.

Hello, I'm Claudia Principal

A certified public accountant originally from Barquisimeto, Venezuela. I graduated with a degree in Accounting and Business Management from Greenville University and earned a master's degree in accounting from Saint Louis University. With experience in both the government and private sectors, I currently lead Claudia Principal & Associates, committed to supporting the Latino community and helping businesses thrive with reliable and customized accounting solutions.
